# References -- GrainSight

## Foundational Papers

1. **Rosin, P. & Rammler, E.** (1933). The laws governing the fineness of
   powdered coal. _Journal of the Institute of Fuel_, 7, 29-36.
   -- The original Rosin-Rammler distribution for particle size analysis.

2. **Bond, F.C.** (1952). The third theory of comminution. _Transactions
   of the AIME_, 193, 484-494.
   -- Bond work index and the significance of D80 in comminution circuit design.

3. **Beucher, S. & Lantuejoul, C.** (1979). Use of watersheds in contour
   detection. _International Workshop on Image Processing_, CCETT/IRISA.
   -- Foundational work on the watershed transform for image segmentation.

4. **Meyer, F.** (1994). Topographic distance and watershed lines.
   _Signal Processing_, 38(1), 113-125.
   -- Formal definition of watershed lines using topographic distance.

## Image-Based Fragmentation Analysis

5. **Maerz, N.H. & Zhou, W.** (1998). WipFrag image-based granulometry
   system. _Proceedings of the FRAGBLAST 5 Workshop on Measurement of
   Blast Fragmentation_, Montreal, 91-99.
   -- Commercial software for muck pile fragmentation from 2D images.

6. **Split Engineering** (2001). _Split Desktop Software Manual_.
   -- Image analysis system for rock fragmentation measurement.

7. **Thurley, M. & Ng, K.** (2008). Identification and sizing of the
   entirely visible rocks from a segmented 3D surface. _Image and Vision
   Computing_, 26(9), 1299-1307.
   -- 3D surface data for rock fragment identification; key reference for
   depth-based grain sizing.

8. **Thurley, M.** (2011). Automated online measurement of limestone
   fragmentation using 3D range data. _6th International Conference on
   3D Body Scanning Technologies_.
   -- Online 3D fragmentation measurement for conveyor belt applications.

## Standards

9. **ISO 13322-1:2014**. _Particle size analysis -- Image analysis methods
   -- Part 1: Static image analysis methods_.
   -- International standard for image-based particle size measurement.

10. **ISO 565:1990**. _Test sieves -- Metal wire cloth, perforated metal
    plate and electroformed sheet -- Nominal sizes of openings_.
    -- Standard sieve series used in physical sieve analysis.

## Recent Deep Learning Approaches

11. **Wang, L. et al.** (2022). A method of blasted rock image segmentation
    based on improved watershed algorithm. _Scientific Reports_, 12, 7408.
    -- Improved watershed with marker control for blasted rock segmentation.

12. **Baek, J. et al.** (2022). Application of deep learning workflow for
    autonomous grain size analysis. _Molecules_, 27(15), 4826.
    -- Deep learning for automated grain size determination.

13. **Hryciw, R.D. & Irsyam, M.** (1993). Determining the grain size
    distribution of granular soils using image analysis. _Geotechnique_.
    -- Early work on digital image-based soil grain sizing.

## Shape Analysis

14. **Mora, C.F. & Kwan, A.K.H.** (2000). Sphericity, shape factor, and
    convexity measurement of coarse aggregate for concrete using digital
    image processing. _Cement and Concrete Research_, 30(3), 351-358.
    -- Shape descriptors for aggregate particles.

## RGB-D Technology

15. **Henry, P. et al.** (2012). RGB-D mapping: Using depth cameras for
    dense 3D modeling of indoor environments. _International Journal of
    Robotics Research_, 31(5), 647-663.
    -- Overview of RGB-D camera technology and applications.
